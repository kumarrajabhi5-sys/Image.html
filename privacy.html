🔒 Privacy Policy

How ExamReady handles your data — short answer: we barely collect any.

📅 Last Updated: December 1, 2024

🛡️ Key point: All image processing in ExamReady happens entirely inside your browser. Your photos, signatures and documents are NEVER uploaded to our servers. We do not see, store or access your images in any way.

1. Introduction

Welcome to ExamReady ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate the website at examready.netlify.app (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service. By using ExamReady, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy has been written to comply with applicable privacy laws and to meet the requirements of Google AdSense and other advertising partners.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

ExamReady does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the tool. You do not need to enter your name, email address, phone number or any other personal details to use our Service.

2.2 Images and Files

When you upload an image (photo, signature, or document), the image is processed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. The image data is never transmitted to our servers, never stored remotely, and never shared with any third party. Once you close or refresh the browser tab, all image data is permanently deleted from your device's memory.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider (Netlify) and analytics tools may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address (anonymised)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Date and time of visit

This information is used only for understanding general website traffic and improving our Service. It cannot be used to personally identify you.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website. These include:

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function correctly. They do not collect any personal information.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymised data about your visit. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

3.3 Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the ExamReady website and tool
  • Understand how users interact with our Service
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Detect and prevent technical issues or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent or share your personal information with any third party for their marketing purposes. We may share anonymised, aggregated data (such as total visitor counts) with our advertising partners. We may also share information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Since all image processing occurs locally in your browser, your sensitive documents and photos are never exposed to the internet through our Service. Our website is served over HTTPS (secure connection) to protect any data transmitted between your browser and our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

ExamReady is intended for use by students preparing for competitive examinations. Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will delete that information.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, such as official exam portals and government websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to opt out of personalised advertising
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information directly, most of these rights would apply to data held by our third-party service providers (such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense).

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We will respond to your inquiry within a reasonable timeframe.
