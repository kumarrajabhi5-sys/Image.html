1. Who We Are

CompressPic (compresspic.in) is a free online image tool that allows users to compress, resize, convert and process images entirely within their web browser. CompressPic is an independent product operated as a personal project.

By using CompressPic, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the service.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data. Here is exactly what happens when you visit CompressPic:

  • Server logs — Your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps may be logged automatically by our hosting provider (Netlify) for security and performance purposes. This is standard web hosting behaviour.
  • Usage analytics — We may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how many people use each tool. This data is aggregated and never tied to individual users.
  • No account data — We do not require registration or login. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers or any other personal information.
Plain language summary: We know people visit our website (like every website does). We do not know who you are, and we have no way to connect your visit to your identity.

3. Your Images & Files

This is the most important section. Your images are never uploaded to our servers.

All image processing on CompressPic — compression, resizing, format conversion, EXIF removal — happens entirely inside your browser using the JavaScript Canvas API and Web APIs. The process works like this:

  • You select an image file on your device
  • Your browser reads the file locally using the File API
  • Processing happens in your browser's memory
  • The result is made available for download directly from your browser
  • No data is sent to any server at any point during this process

You can verify this by opening your browser's developer tools, going to the Network tab, and observing that no image data is transmitted when you use our tools.

We do not store, analyse, share, or have access to any images you process on CompressPic.
